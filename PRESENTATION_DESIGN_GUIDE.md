## Presentation Tips

### Timing (20-minute pitch)
- Slides 1-3: 3 minutes (Problem/Solution)
- Slides 4-7: 5 minutes (Product/Tech)
- Slides 8-11: 5 minutes (Business/Traction)
- Slides 12-15: 4 minutes (Team/Financials)
- Slides 16-22: 3 minutes (Demo/CTA)

### Speaker Notes
- Keep to 2-3 key points per slide
- Use slide content as visual aid, not script
- Tell stories (user testimonials, team journey)
- Practice demo beforehand

### Q&A Preparation
- Common questions:
  - "How do you compete with Wise?"
  - "What's your user acquisition strategy?"
  - "Why Stellar vs Ethereum?"
  - "What's the regulatory risk?"
- Backup slides ready
